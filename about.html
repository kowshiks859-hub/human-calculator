About Human Battery

Human Battery is a playful wellness tracker designed for fun, mindfulness, and healthy self-reflection. It offers a quick, interactive way to check in with your daily habits and see how they impact your overall mood and energy projection.

⚠️ Friendly Disclaimer

This estimator is intended solely for recreational visualization and is not a medical diagnostic tool. Percentages are estimated based on simple input math, and are not substitute for professional medical care, diagnosis, or health advisory.

How Your Battery Works

We combine your metrics into a single score using a weighted wellness model:

😴

Sleep (40% Weight)

Calculated against an optimal 8-hour target. Quality sleep acts as the primary recovery stage for your battery.

💧

Water Intake (20% Weight)

Targets a baseline of 3 Liters daily. Hydration keeps cognitive pathways and metabolic functions clear.

😊

Current Mood (20% Weight)

Measures mental energy. Your emotional outlook directly regulates the rate of energy expenditure.

🏃

Exercise (20% Weight)

Targets a baseline of 45 minutes of movement. Cardio and strength training boost cardiovascular performance and build energy stamina.
